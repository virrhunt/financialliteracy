TKTK financial term Definition

The graph above compares the total global asset growth of mutual funds and exchange-traded funds over a period between 1998 and 2023. Though the two investments are very similar, the chart demonstrates the recent growth of popularity in ETFs, which differ from mutual funds in their trading window and management style. While mutual funds can only be traded once day after the markets close, ETFs can be bought and sold throughout the day, similar to regular stocks. Over the past 20 years, both types of investments have seen significant growth due their associations with low risk and diversification, making them safer options for new investors.
